Welcome to the World of the Big Three

In the vast universe of anime and manga, three series stood above all others during the golden age of Shonen Jump magazine: One Piece, Naruto, and Bleach. These legendary series, collectively known as "The Big Three," revolutionized the anime industry and captured the hearts of millions worldwide.

Cultural Impact

The Big Three have left an indelible mark on both Japanese and global pop culture. These series have:

  • Inspired countless other anime and manga creators
  • Generated billions in revenue through manga, anime, and merchandise
  • Created massive international fan communities
  • Introduced millions of people to anime and manga
  • Revolutionized storytelling in long-running series
